About

Lexi Scovil is a professional event rider in the Ocala area, offering lessons, training, and sales. A former rider for William Fox-Pitt, Lexi competes internationally at the 4* level.

Learn

Lexi is available for lessons for all levels of riders at her farm or yours in the Ocala area. She has learned from many world-renowned riders and trainers through her life and loves watching riders progress for competitive success and their own enjoyment.
